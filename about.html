Our Story

We Have Been Serving Southern California Since 1958

Angels Bail Bonds is a family-owned business established in 1958 with one mission: to help families through one of the most stressful experiences they will ever face. Over 65 years later, we're still here — still family-owned, still answering every call personally, still getting people home.

When you call us, you speak to a licensed bail agent, not a call center. We treat every client with care and professionalism.
